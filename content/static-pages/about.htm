Diversity Hub is a think tank, the axis of which is thematic diversity in the workplace.

The aim of the Diversity Hub is the integration of environmental experts around the idea of diversity, the search for practical and effective solutions in this area, and start a public discussion on topics related to diversity with the participation of representatives of diverse backgrounds: academics and practitioners - representatives of business and business environment institutions, public institutions and the media.

We promote and publicize the idea of diversity management among Polish entrepreneurs, as well as create their own innovative solutions to improve the effective implementation of diversity management in organizations, in particular its dimensions, such as age, situation of women and men at work, disability, interculturalism, etc.. We are moving well many other important topics related to eg. the area of work-life balance, health, including mental health workers, and finally with the rights of the employee (bullying
at work).

The main axis of activity Diversity Hub are cyclical events with the participation of experts in the field of diversity management representing various fields - thanks to discussing the problem we combine with each other, sometimes divergent approaches to the problem presented by the world of science and business.

The Diversity Hub is also looking for practical solutions, are working together on projects in the field of diversity management, employers solve specific problems in the area of diversity.
